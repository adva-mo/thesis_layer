# Thesis Layer

**Research → Investment Thesis → Multi-Platform Content**

Thesis Layer is an AI-powered content system that transforms raw real estate research into a structured investment thesis, then expresses that thesis across every content format.

One property.
One thesis.
Many surfaces.

---

## The Problem

Most real estate content starts with marketing.

Thesis Layer starts with a question:

> What is the actual investment?

Not:

* luxury
* premium
* amazing opportunity

Instead:

* What is the bet?
* What must be true for it to work?
* What is the risk?
* What is already proven?

The system builds content from those answers.

## Video Production Pipeline

Reels are not generated as standalone videos.

Each reel is produced from the underlying investment thesis.

```text
Investment Thesis
    ↓
Reel Script
    ↓
Voice Generation (ElevenLabs)
    ↓
Word-Level Alignment
    ↓
Asset Collection
    ↓
AI Video Generation (Kling)
    ↓
Scene Assembly
    ↓
Subtitle Rendering
    ↓
Final Reel
```

Current capabilities:

* Hebrew + English support
* Mixed RTL/LTR subtitle rendering
* Word-level subtitle highlighting
* AI-generated video scenes
* Programmatic timeline and CTA scenes
* Automated reel assembly and rendering

## Core Principles

### One Thesis, Many Surfaces

Every piece of content expresses the same investment argument.

Only the depth changes.

### Research First

Facts before narrative.

Missing information stays missing.

### Risk Is Required

Every thesis includes downside.

Credibility depends on it.

### Final Impression Rule

Content never ends on unresolved negativity.

The goal is not optimism.

The goal is clarity.
