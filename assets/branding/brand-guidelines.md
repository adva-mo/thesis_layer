# Brand Guidelines

Reference document for visual and verbal identity. Keep this consistent across all content.

---

## Brand Positioning

**One-liner:** The smartest way to track Dubai real estate investment opportunities.

**For Israelis:** "המדריך החכם להשקעה בנדל"ן דובאי"
**For English speakers:** "Curated Dubai real estate intelligence for serious investors"

**We are NOT:** a brokerage, an agency, a listing site, or a lifestyle brand.
**We ARE:** a curated intelligence layer between raw market data and investor decisions.

---

## Narrator Voice

The narrator is a **woman**. All first-person VO copy must use feminine verb forms in Hebrew (e.g., "בוחנת" not "בוחן", "משקיעה" not "משקיע"). This applies to reel scripts, hooks, and any spoken content. The ElevenLabs voice is female — masculine verb forms will be a mismatch between text and audio.

## Visual Identity Notes

### Colors (guidelines for canva / design tools)

- **Primary:** Deep navy `#0A1628` — trustworthy, premium, analytical
- **Accent:** Gold `#C9A84C` — Dubai reference, premium without being tacky
- **Background:** Off-white `#F8F6F1` — clean, readable, non-harsh
- **Text:** Near-black `#1A1A2E`

### Typography (for designed posts)

- **Headings:** Clean sans-serif (Montserrat, Inter — or primary language equivalent per `primary_language.md`)
- **Body:** Readable sans-serif (Inter, Open Sans — or primary language equivalent per `primary_language.md`)
- **Data callouts:** Mono or bold — make numbers stand out

### Image style

- Architecture: clean, wide shots — no fish-eye distortion
- Lifestyle: understated — no champagne, no yacht, no Lamborghini
- Maps and area context shots: highly preferred
- Data visualizations: simple charts, no cluttered infographics
- No stock photos of fake-smiling families in front of houses

## What to Avoid (Visual)

- Fake "Dubai skyline at sunset" stock photos used by every competitor
- Oversaturated gold and diamond imagery
- Stock photos of luxury penthouses that don't match the actual project
- Cluttered slides with 8 bullet points and 3 fonts

## Voice & Tone

### Sound like:

- A smart friend who invests in real estate and shares what actually matters
- Calm confidence — you know your data, you don't need to shout
- Analytical but human — numbers with context, not numbers as noise
- Curious-inviting — open the conversation, don't close the sale
- Observant, slightly contrarian, operator-minded

### Never sound like:

- A sales pitch ("this is a once-in-a-lifetime opportunity!")
- A luxury influencer ("the lifestyle you deserve")
- A generic AI ("The market is experiencing unprecedented growth")
- Fake urgency ("only 3 units left — call NOW!")
- An investment memo being narrated ("the thesis is supported by the following signals") — architecture informs the reasoning, never appears in the sentence
- Perfect marketing structure or excessive bullet formatting

### Writing behaviors:

The content should occasionally pause on an insight, challenge assumptions, and contrast hype vs reality.

Goal: "smart market observer" — not "AI-generated real estate marketing."
