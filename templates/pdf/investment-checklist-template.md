```markdown
# Dubai Off-Plan Investment Checklist
## Before Signing Any Off-Plan Contract

Use this for every project you're evaluating. If you can't answer a question — that's your research starting point.

---

## 1. Developer Due Diligence

- [ ] Developer is registered with RERA (rera.ae) with no open complaints
- [ ] At least 3 prior projects completed and delivered
- [ ] Average delay on prior projects is known and explained
- [ ] This specific project has a DLD project registration number
- [ ] An Escrow account is registered with DLD for this project

---

## 2. Location Due Diligence

- [ ] You know the specific community and subarea (not just "Dubai")
- [ ] Existing infrastructure confirmed: roads, retail, services
- [ ] Current rental yields for comparable units in the area are known (via Bayut, Property Finder)
- [ ] Future development pipeline for the area is understood (metro, commercial, community)
- [ ] Typical tenant profile for the area is understood (expats, families, professionals)

---

## 3. Payment Plan Evaluation

- [ ] Total downpayment known in AED — not just percentage
- [ ] Full payment schedule documented in writing
- [ ] Late payment penalties are understood
- [ ] Exit options before handover are understood (resale, cancellation terms)
- [ ] Flip feasibility and transaction costs are known

---

## 4. Legal and Regulatory Checks

- [ ] SPA (Sales Purchase Agreement) has been read in full
- [ ] Independent lawyer (not the developer's) has reviewed the contract
- [ ] DLD Escrow protection in case of developer insolvency is understood
- [ ] Annual Service Charge (maintenance fee) post-handover is known
- [ ] Short-term rental restrictions (if any) are confirmed
- [ ] Oqood registration (DLD interim title) will be issued post-signing

---

## 5. Personal Fit

- [ ] Downpayment is liquid capital — not money I need within 3 years
- [ ] I can hold this investment for at least 3-5 years
- [ ] I understand off-plan is illiquid until handover
- [ ] Exit strategy is defined: rental income / resale / personal use
- [ ] I have or can access: lawyer, accountant, property manager

---

## Score: ______ / 25

- 22-25: Ready to proceed with confidence
- 17-21: 2-3 open questions — resolve before signing
- Below 17: More research needed

---

*This checklist is a thinking tool — not legal or financial advice. Consult professionals before any transaction.*
```
